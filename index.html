What is Agriculture?

Agriculture is the science and art of cultivating plants and livestock. It forms the backbone of the world's food supply and plays a crucial role in our economy.

Key Facts

  • Over 60% of the world population relies on agriculture for livelihood.
  • Sustainable farming helps protect our environment.
  • Modern tech (like drones and AI) is reshaping agriculture.
